Planning

Last updated: June 22nd

Process overview

The planning workflow is about processing a TV advertising plan (simply referred to as plan), and generating the corresponding order forms (orders) which can then be sent to various sales houses, thus eventually converting money into the TV advertising was planned out.
A plan comprises campaigns, and campaigns are made up of flights. These groupings primarily serve management and reporting purposes, effectively organizing plan lines.
It is important to note that a plan may validly contain only a single campaign, and a campaign may likewise include just one flight. Once a plan is approved, it is handed over (assigned) to a buyer. To make this delegation simpler for planners, a third group exists - supervisors, who are responsible for allocating a buyer for a plan.
